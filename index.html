Why Stashback

Built differently from other backup apps

Most backup tools store your data in plaintext and replicate to a single region. Stashback doesn't.

Encrypted before it leaves your store

Your backups are encrypted with AES-256-GCM at the application layer before upload. That means your cloud storage provider — not just any attacker — cannot read your backup data. Most backup apps rely on storage-provider encryption alone, where the provider holds the keys.

See what changes before you commit

A restore is not a gamble. Stashback's dry-run preview shows you exactly which records will be created, updated, or left unchanged — before a single live record is touched. Restore with confidence, not crossed fingers.

Redundancy across two continents

Pro plans replicate every backup to both a US and an EU data centre in real time. A regional outage, a provider incident, or a corrupted object in one region never leaves you without a recoverable copy.
