Features

A single app covers every corner of your store — not just products.

🗄️

Full store coverage

Products, collections, orders, customers, themes, pages, blogs, navigation, metaobjects, files, discounts — all in one backup.

⏰

Automated schedules

Set daily or hourly backups and forget about it. Stashback runs automatically in the background on your schedule.

⚡

One-click restore

Something went wrong? Select a backup and restore your entire store or specific resource types in minutes, not days.

Dual-region storage

🔔

Failure notifications

Get emailed instantly if a backup fails, so you're never left without protection without knowing about it.

Cross-store restore

Moving to a new Shopify store? Claim your old backups and restore your data into the new store seamlessly.
