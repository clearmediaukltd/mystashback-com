Backup Coverage

What Stashback backs up

Coverage depends on your plan. Here's exactly what's included at each tier, whether it can be restored, and any limitations worth knowing.

All plans Starter Growth Pro

Resource Restorable Notes
Products ✓ Variants, images, media, metafields, and SEO. Images are downloaded into the backup so they're preserved even if the original is later deleted.
Collections ✓ Smart (rule-based) and manual collections, including product membership. Publication channels (e.g. Online Store) are matched by name on restore.
Pages ✓ Full HTML content, SEO title and description, template suffix.
Blogs & articles ✓ Blog structure and all articles, including author, tags, featured image, and metafields. Article images are downloaded into the backup.
Navigation ✓ Up to 3 levels of menu items. Items are restored as generic links — resource-typed items (e.g. direct product or collection links) will need re-linking after restore.
Policies ✓ All shop policies (refund, privacy, shipping, etc.).
Redirects ✓ All URL redirects. Complete coverage.
Customers ✓ Profile, addresses, tags, metafields, and marketing consent. Addresses are only written for customers being created (not updated). Customers must reset their password on first login — passwords are never exposed by Shopify's API.
Orders Read-only Order data is captured for reference and export. Shopify does not allow historical orders to be re-created via its API, so orders cannot be restored into your store.

Growth and above Growth Pro

Resource Restorable Notes
Metaobjects ✓ Both definitions (types, fields, validations) and all entries. Complete coverage.
Discounts ✓ Percentage, fixed amount, buy X get Y, and free shipping discounts. Product and customer targeting is not restored — discounts apply store-wide after restore. Expired discounts are skipped. Discounts managed by apps (Discount Functions) are backed up but cannot be restored.
Themes ✓ All theme files — Liquid templates, JSON settings, CSS, JS, and image assets. Restored as a new unpublished theme so your live storefront is never overwritten.
Draft orders ✓ Open draft orders including line items, discounts, customer details, and shipping. Drafts that have already been completed (converted to orders) are skipped on restore.

Pro only Pro

Resource Restorable Notes
Files & media ✓ All files in your Shopify Files library — images, documents, and videos. Videos are restored by re-importing from the original source URL; if that URL is no longer accessible the video cannot be restored.
Shipping profiles ✓ Zones, countries, and flat-rate shipping methods. Carrier-calculated rates are not included. Product-to-profile assignments are not captured.
Locations ✓ Location name, address, and active status. Locations are matched by name on restore. Deactivation is not applied during restore to avoid leaving a store with no active location.
Inventory levels ✓ Available stock quantity per variant per location. Only "available" quantity is restored — committed and incoming stock counts are not. Variants are matched by product handle and option values.

Not included

The following are not currently backed up by Stashback:

  • Gift cards — gift card balances and expiry can be captured, but original codes are masked by Shopify and cannot be preserved.
  • Third-party app data — data owned by other apps (reviews, loyalty points, subscriptions) is not accessible to Stashback.
  • Shopify Payments & transactions — payment records and transaction history are read-only and cannot be exported or restored.
  • Markets, catalogs, and B2B (Companies) — niche features; not yet covered.
  • Shop settings — currency, timezone, and other store settings are readable but cannot be restored via Shopify's API.
API limitations: Some restore gaps above — such as orders and customer passwords — are hard limits imposed by Shopify's API, not Stashback limitations. They cannot be worked around. See the restore limitations section of the Help page for the full list.
